Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

CreatorFlow ("we", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data from TikTok APIs.

1. Data Collection

We only access your TikTok username and public profile info via user.info.basic and upload videos via video.upload with your explicit consent.

2. Data Usage

We do not sell your data. TikTok data is only used to facilitate the video publishing process through our dashboard.

3. Data Deletion

Users can revoke access at any time through TikTok settings or by contacting us.
